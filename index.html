Karen Pickering received her RHIT (Registered Health Information Technician) certificate in 1990 and her CCS (Certified Coding Specialist) in 1994. She is proficient in ICD-9 and CPT coding and has been certified as an ICD-10 trainer through the American Health Information Management Association (AHIMA). Ms. Pickering is a 30 year veteran and expert in coding, auditing, and mentoring to healthcare facilities and Health Information Management professionals. She performs comprehensive coding reviews to assure correct, compliant coding practices in a multitude of healthcare settings including acute hospitals, emergency rooms, outpatient clinics, rehabilitation facilities and physician offices. Ms. Pickering has served as the Secretary for the Inland Area Health Information Association from 2011 to 2012.
